We're excited to welcome you to [Community Over Code Asia 2026](https://asia.communityovercode.org) — the premier open-source event in Asia, hosted by the Apache Software Foundation. Taking place in Beijing from August 7–9, 2026, this in-person gathering is your chance to dive into the latest innovations, learn from domain experts, and connect with the global open-source community.

Apache Cloudberry will be featured across **seven sessions**, spanning topics from new releases and query optimization to community building and network performance. In addition, we are hosting a **booth** and an **in-person community gathering** during the conference — there are plenty of opportunities to meet the team and fellow community members!

## Featured Sessions

Here is the list of Apache Cloudberry related sessions accepted for Community Over Code Asia 2026:

| Track | Session | Speaker(s) |
|-------|---------|------------|
| Data Lake & Data Warehouse | [What's new in Apache Cloudberry (Incubating) 2.1 and 3.0](https://asia.communityovercode.org/sessions/datalake-1213358.html) | Max Yang |
| Data Storage & Computing | [When the Optimizer Lies: Debugging Cross-Slice Execution in Apache Cloudberry](https://asia.communityovercode.org/sessions/datastorage-1213149.html) | Alena Rybakina |
| Incubator | [Beyond Incubation: Building a Sustainable Apache Community with Cloudberry](https://asia.communityovercode.org/sessions/incubator-1210256.html) | Dianjin Wang |
| Incubator | [Build Once, Run on Any Linux: SynxDB CE for Apache Cloudberry (Incubating)](https://asia.communityovercode.org/sessions/incubator-1212002.html) | Shine Zhang & Ed Espino |
| Observability | [Apache Cloudberry: gathering statistics for queries executed by multiple PostgreSQL instances](https://asia.communityovercode.org/sessions/observability-1195519.html) | Leonid Borchuk |
| OLAP & Data Analysis | [Deep Dive into Parallel Query Execution Solutions for Apache Cloudberry](https://asia.communityovercode.org/sessions/olap-1210336.html) | Rose Duan |
| OLAP & Data Analysis | [We tried DPDK for network but failed. Why and what we have learned from this?](https://asia.communityovercode.org/sessions/olap-1195516.html) | Leonid Borchuk |

Congratulations to all speakers — we can't wait to see your talks!

## Visit the Cloudberry Booth

The Apache Cloudberry community will be onsite with a dedicated booth. Drop by to meet the team, ask questions, check out live demos, and grab some awesome Cloudberry swag!

## Community Gathering

We are also organizing an in-person Apache Cloudberry community gathering during the conference. The discussion will focus on Cloudberry's incubation, graduation, and community development. If you have topics you'd like to discuss, we'd love to hear from you — come join the conversation!

Ready to join? [Register](https://asia.communityovercode.org/#register) now to attend Community Over Code Asia 2026 in person. We look forward to seeing you in Beijing!
